Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how the New Year Blessing Messages App ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your data.

Information We Collect

The App does not require user registration and does not collect or store personal data directly. However, the App integrates with Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect information per their privacy policy.

  • Usage Data: Anonymous information about how the App is used, such as feature interactions and time spent.
  • Device Information: Non-identifiable details about your device, such as operating system version and app version.

How We Use Your Information

Any information collected is used solely to:

  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • Improve app performance and functionality.
  • Provide a better user experience by analyzing anonymous usage trends.

The App does not use your information for purposes beyond these outlined uses.

Sharing Blessing Messages

The App enables you to share New Year blessing messages via third-party platforms such as WeChat, SMS etc. When sharing, these platforms may collect information per their privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for further information.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates with Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads. Please refer to Google AdMob's Privacy Policy for details on their data practices.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your information and employ reasonable measures to protect any data collected. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute data security.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take immediate steps to delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly.
